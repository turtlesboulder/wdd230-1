Games

The Virtual Garden is a place to relax, so why not play some games? Studies have found that playing video games reduces the risk of boredom by 95%! If you are one of those 5% of people, then you can always head over to the garden and drag some plants around.

Robot Finds Kitten

This is a game where everything is represented by characters, and you (the robot) try to find the character that represents kitten. The little characters have funny messages when you walk over them. This game is great because you can just read stuff and its not complicated or difficult.

Plant Clicker

You click a plant and a number goes up. Very satisfying. I'm sure you've heard of other clicker games before, like tree clicker. Or beaver clicker!
