Target Audience

A fair warning- This is really long because when I was making the content for this website, I looked up how to construct personas, and landed on a website that established a super long and detailed process. I realise now that it was full of metaphorical feces, likley someone just writing a soulless 'buisinessy' article, but it fooled me regardless. I'm already too commited into these way-too-detailed personas to turn back now, however.

The target audience of the site are buisiness owners who live in or near Hacker Valley. A secondary audience might be common people who live in or are researching hacker valley, and are interested in what the chamber is doing. Buisiness owners will likely be older, male, white, educated, and fond of activities such as golfing, fishing, hiking, and ranching, given the rural area (Hacker valley is in west virginia). They will likely own buisiness such as logging, agriculture/fishing, and ones found everywhere such as construction, carpentry, or a restauraunt owner.

-- Persona 1 --

What is the age of your person?

40

What is the gender of your person?

Male

What is the highest level of education this person has recieved?

Master's degree from the east in 'Agriculture engineering technology'.

How much work experience does this person have?

They have worked since they were 14 doing manual labor, and they got educated when they turned 18. Afterwords they worked on big farms around the country until they started their own logging company 5 years ago.

Why will they come to this site?

They are interested in making buisiness connections and joining a group of similar successful people.

Where (or from whom) else is this person getting information about your programs or services?

They know about chambers of commerce, and have been thinking about looking the local one up for awhile.

When and where will this user access the site?

They access it either on their phone or laptop computer. Likely in the morning as they think about work activities, or perhaps in the evening after they get home.

What technological devices does your person use on a regular basis?

They are able to use a wide variety of devices and use different buisiness softwares on a regular basis, such as finance management applications or filling out government paperwork online.

How much time does your person spend brousing the web each day?

Not very often. They are productive with their time and know what they need. They spend their free time offline.

What is your person motivated by?

They want to accumulate a lot of money, and they also are social and like to make aquaintances and friends.

What are they looking for?

They are looking for buisiness connections with people, and to join a network of successful people like them.

What are their needs?

In general, they would need to resolve various issues that appear in their buisiness. Perhaps legal troubles, perhaps they need to hire someone new in management. They would need ways to relax out in nature.

-- Persona 2 --

What is the age of your person?

50

What is the gender of your person?

Male

How much work experience does this person have?

They have worked since they were very young doing odd jobs, but never consistently. They got a full time job at a power plant but always wanted to start their own restaurant. They started it up 25 years ago and were surpisingly successful, and now have multiple locations around west virginia.

Why will they come to this site?

They know other buisiness owners who are part of the chamber of commerce, and have been recommended to join it by them.

Where (or from whom) else is this person getting information about your programs or services?

See above

When and where will this user access the site?

They will access it on a desktop computer at their 'office'. It will likely be in the morning as they begin their work activities.

What technological devices does your person use on a regular basis?

They don't use technology very much, mostly just using their work desktop. They have a cell phone, but don't often use the internet on it, although they do use it to watch youtube.

How much time does your person spend brousing the web each day?

Sometimes in the evenings for entertainment. Probably about an hour a day.

What is your person motivated by?

They want to grow their buisiness and continue to open up new locations. They also sports games and love free weekends to watch them at the stadium.

What are they looking for?

They are looking for buisiness connections as well as opportunities to learn more about how 'buisiness' works by talking with their peers.

What are their needs?

In general, they need to be more skilled/involved in the buisiness world as their buisiness is becoming less and less 'small'. They considered selling it, but it was their dream for it to be big and they are happy that it is.
